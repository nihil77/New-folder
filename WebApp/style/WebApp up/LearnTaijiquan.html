Whats with Taijiquan?

Firts Let's defined whats Taijiquan. Taijiquan is a traditional practive that charactrized bu relaxed, circular movements that works in concert with breath regulation and the cultivation of a righteous and neutral mind. It is also system of maritial arts, health exercise and spiritual cultivation of qi to nourish the body with abbreviated 'Taiji'.
Taijiquan comes from the word "Tai" （太）means “Supreme”, “Ji” （极or Chi) means “Boundary” and the word “Quan” （拳or Chuan) means “fist” or “movement”. Together the term “Taijiquan” implies a method of movement to cultivate
